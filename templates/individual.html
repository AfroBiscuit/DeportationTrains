Their Story

Born in China, came to USA via Mexico after working as a merchant and labourer for several years. Begins working for the US Army Quartermasters but is arrested for theft in 1919. He is then held in the San Antonio County Jail before being transfered to the Southwestern Insane Asylum. Became a Public Charge and embarked on a deportation train bound West on January 2nd, 1920. The train passed through ... ... ... before arriving in San Francisco, where Luey Mo was then deported to Hong Kong via Hawaii, Japan, and China.
